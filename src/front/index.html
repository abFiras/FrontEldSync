Medical Departments

Our Platform covers various medical departments, providing consultations and resources for heart health, neurological conditions, liver health, pediatric care, and eye care, all accessible remotely for user convenience.

  • Cardiology
  • Neurology
  • Hepatology
  • Hematology
  • Eye Care

Cardiology

Our Platform provides comprehensive cardiology services, including remote monitoring of heart health, consultation with cardiologists, and access to personalized treatment plans for heart conditions.

Neurology

With our app, users can receive expert neurology consultations, track neurological symptoms, and access resources for managing conditions like migraines, epilepsy, or Parkinson's disease.

Hepatology

Users can benefit from hepatology services on our app, which include virtual consultations with liver specialists, monitoring of liver health parameters, and guidance on managing liver diseases such as hepatitis or fatty liver disease.

Hematology

Our platform provides extensive hematology services, offering remote consultations with hematologists and personalized treatment plans for various blood-related disorders.

Eye Care

Users can access comprehensive eye care services through our app, including virtual consultations with ophthalmologists, tracking of vision health metrics, and ordering prescription glasses or contact lenses conveniently.
